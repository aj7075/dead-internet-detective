🕵️ Dead Internet Detective

An RL environment for training LLMs to investigate disinformation through multi-hop reasoning over a synthetic internet.

The Environment

Most disinformation detection is classification — read text, output a label. That misses the point. Sophisticated fakes fail not because they sound wrong, but because their citations lead nowhere, their authors don't exist, and their domains were registered last week. You can't catch any of that by reading the headline. You have to investigate.

This environment gives an LLM agent a claim and a set of dossier URLs — entry points into a fully synthetic internet. The agent has 12 tools, a case file to maintain, and a step budget. When it's ready, it files a report. A five-component reward function scores not just the verdict but the quality of the investigation.

Reward Function

ComponentWeightWhat it measures
Verdict accuracy30%Right conclusion?
Evidence chain quality25%Did you cite the relevant pages?

Internal consistency15%Does reasoning match evidence?
Step efficiency10%Solved without burning the budget?
